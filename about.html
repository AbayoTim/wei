About Us

Empowering women, transforming communities since our founding.

Our Mission

Women Empowerment Initiatives (WEI) focuses on enhancing women's autonomy and capabilities. We address how women have fewer opportunities for economic participation than men, less access to basic and higher education.

Our work emphasizes strengthening women's decision-making authority, resource access, and ability to create meaningful change in their lives and communities.

Contact Us

Our Vision

A society where women are empowered, have equal opportunities, and actively participate in all aspects of community development.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: Building capacity and confidence
  • Equality: Advocating for equal rights and opportunities
  • Community: Working together for collective growth
  • Sustainability: Creating lasting impact
  • Integrity: Operating with transparency and accountability

Areas of Operation
