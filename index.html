Our Programs

WEI operates sustainable programs benefiting women, youth, and children across Dodoma City Council, Chamwino District Council, and Bahi District Council in Tanzania.

Why Choose WEI

We are committed to creating lasting change in the lives of women and communities across Tanzania through sustainable, community-driven programs.

1

Community-Driven Approach

We work directly with local communities to understand and address their specific needs.

2

Sustainable Programs

Our programs focus on long-term impact through skills training, education support, and economic empowerment.

3

Strong Partnerships

We collaborate with ACWW, ViiV Healthcare, and government entities to maximize our impact.

4

Holistic Development

We address multiple aspects including health, education, economic opportunities, and leadership.
